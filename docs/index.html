FLARESat - Our Custom Satellite

Advanced Fire Detection Technology

FLARESat is our custom-designed satellite specifically engineered for real-time wildfire detection and monitoring from Low Earth Orbit.

Detection Range

6km radius coverage

Orbital Altitude

400-600km LEO

Solar Panels

High-efficiency deployable arrays

Communication

Real-time data transmission

Equipped with thermal imaging sensors and advanced AI algorithms, FLARESat can detect fire signatures and coordinate with nearby satellites for multi-point verification before alerting ground stations.
